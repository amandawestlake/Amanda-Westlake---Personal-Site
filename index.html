Hey there! My name is Amanda, and I study Computer Science and Political Science at Tufts University. I've worked as a field organizer and intern on various political campaigns at the national, state, and local levels, and am currently the managing editor of the Tufts Observer, a biweekly magazine and Tufts's oldest publication. I'm interested in the intersections of politics and technology, tech for good, journalism, and climate justice advocacy. In my free time, I enjoy reading, crossword puzzles, ceramics, Tetris, and antiquing.

Some of my proudest accomplishments are my work as an organizer on the Ed Markey campaign and as a co-founder and leader of Students for Markey, and my writing and editing with the Observer. Watch my video with Senator Markey and learn about the goal thermometer I created! At Tufts, I'm also a hub coordinator of the Sunrise Movement and a teaching assistant for Data Structures (CS15).
